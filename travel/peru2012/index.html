Fun Facts

Connect with me via Social Networks

To be able to conquer the Inca Trail, I got very serious about weight loss and fitness, losing approximately 30 lbs while training for the hike (and I'm glad I did!)

Bullet Ants in the Amazon are named as such because being bitten by them is as painful as being shot with a bullet. We saw a whole nest of them. Our guides were keen to show us them, but I ran away as fast as I could. :)

Upon seeing Iguazu Falls for the first time, Eleanor Roosevelt exclaimed, "Poor Niagara!"

Buenos Aires has the only Kosher McDonald's outside of Israel. Yep, I defintely went there.
