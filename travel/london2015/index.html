Fun Facts

Connect with me via Social Networks

On the way to Israel from London, I stopped in Istanbul to experience the lounge at the airport. It is world-renowned and was absolutely amazing -- like visiting a Turkish market with tons of local food!

This trip was amidst the lead-up to the Warner Bros. release of the Batkid Begins documentary that I "star" in, so I had some fun opportunities. I interviewed for the Batkid press kit up at Warner Bros. Leavesden Studios. What a blast!

My most favourite activities were: visiting the Borough Market on a Saturday, the restaurant DIshoom in Covent Garden, walking the Regent's Canal from Angel Station to London Fields, and hiking in Hampstead Heath.
