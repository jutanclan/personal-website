This epic trip was an "incentive trip" from Michal's work, a bonus for her performance last year! Incredible. It was very special to be able to go with her.

We picked "Pre-QingMing Longjing (Dragon Well) Tea" from a village surrounding Hangzhou, China. This is one of the most sought-after green teas in the world and it tastes like a dream!

Purple Mountain in Nanjing was one of the most beautiful and scenic days of the trip. Nanjing is the Fortune-500 capital of China, but it is also known as the greenest city in China.
