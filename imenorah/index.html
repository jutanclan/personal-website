iMenorah and iMenorah HD
For the Jew far from home!

Take part in the ancient tradition of lighting the Menorah with this simple and beautiful application. Great for traveling, college dorms and kids!

Features:

  • Easy to use
  • Appropriate for all ages
  • Light candles with the touch of your finger
  • Automatically shows the right number of candles per day of Chanukah / Hanukkah
  • Beautiful graphics and animation
  • Blessings sung in Hebrew
  • Candles melt down over 30 minutes
  • Fully internationalized
  • iPhone® 4/4S Retina screen support
  • iPad® Support (with iMenorah HD)

Charitable Donation:

By downloading iMenorah and iMenorah HD, you are also supporting San Francisco's local community. In previous years, we have donated 10% of the proceeds to the JCCSF, and the San Francisco Food Bank.
