iMenorah & iMenorah HD

In Fall 2008, as the Apple iTunes App Store was growing in popularity, my good friend Matthew Parrott and I teamed up to create iMenorah. Our goal was to create a well-designed iPhone app which would be accessible to people of all ages, helping them celebrate the Jewish holiday of Chanukah while on-the-go or traveling.

It was a great experience creating this app together and we’ve really enjoyed the stories that have come from people using it around the world.

In 2011, we updated iMenorah for iPhone to support the Retina screen resolution, and also created iMenorah HD for iPad.
